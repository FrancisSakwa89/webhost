Our Story

Considering that people are often faced by time and resource consuming processes to find a suitable/desired healthcare practitioner. i.e relying on references from friends and family; making countless calls and visits to multiple hospitals.
A team of five decided to innovate myDaktari as avenue by which patients can be united to healthcare professionals, particularly specialty medics.

WEBHOST is an app created by a team of five students from Moringa school who include:

Cynthia Muriithi
Elias Kanoga
Francis Sakwa
Vynnel Omondi
Came together while at Moringa School to conceptualize this pivotal healthcare innovation.
It is our hope that millions of Kenya benefit from the service, patients and healthcare practitioners alike.
